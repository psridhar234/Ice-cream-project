Features

  1. On the home page, there is a search bar where you can search for the ice cream flavor you want. If the ice cream flavor is available, an image of the ice cream will show up under the search bar. If the ice cream flavor isn't available, it will display "no results".
  2. On the Order page Order page, choose between delivery or pickup. Then fill in the Billing Address, Delivery Address, Email for Receipt, choose to pay online or pick up option, select credit card type, and enter the credit card number. If you click the submit button without completing the form, you will receive an error pop-up message indicating that delivery or pickup is required, billing address is required, contact number is required, email is required, and payment method selection is required.
  3. Also on Order pagethe Order page, if the user doesn't enter the correct number of digits for the selected credit card type, an error message will pop up stating that the credit card number must meet the required digits for that type.
  4. On the Registration page, if the user presses the submit button leaving the form blank, they will receive a pop-up error message stating that email is required, password is required, username is required, and postcode is required.
  5. Also on the Registration page, if the user doesn't meet the minimum number of digits for the password and postcode, they will receive a pop-up error message stating that the password must be at least 9 characters long and the postcode must be 4 digits.
